# 🤝 Contributing to Project Theta

First off — **thank you**. Every contribution, no matter how small, moves us closer to a world where AI knowledge is truly accessible to everyone.

---

## 📐 Our Standards

All contributions must align with the **"It's Just AI"** philosophy:
- ✅ Accessible language — no unnecessary jargon
- ✅ Practical and actionable — real-world relevance
- ✅ Accurate — factually sound and up-to-date
- ✅ Non-promotional — no tool/product advertisements disguised as education

---

## 🛠️ Ways to Contribute

### 1. Add a Resource
Have a great tool, article, paper, or video? Add it to the appropriate file under `/resources/`.

### 2. Add a Prompt
Contribute a useful, tested AI prompt to `/prompts/`. Include what model it's best for and the use case.

### 3. Fix / Improve Existing Content
Typos, outdated links, better explanations — all welcome. Just open a PR.

### 4. Add to AI History
Know a milestone in AI history we're missing? Add it to `/history/ai-timeline.md`.

### 5. Build a Learning Path
Have a structured path you've validated? Add it under `/syllabi/`.

---

## 🔁 The Pull Request Process

1. **Fork** the repository
2. **Create a branch**: `git checkout -b add/your-contribution-name`
3. **Make your changes** — keep edits focused and scoped
4. **Write a clear PR description**: What did you add? Why is it valuable?
5. **Submit the PR** against the `main` branch
6. A maintainer will review within **7 days**

## ❌ What We Don't Accept

- Paid course promotions or affiliate links
- Content behind paywalls
- Misleading or sensationalized AI claims
- Plagiarized content
