# 🟢 Beginner Learning Path: AI Literacy

Welcome to Project Theta. This path is designed for someone who has heard about AI but doesn't know where to start. No coding, no math, just pure literacy.

---

## 📅 Week 1: The "It's Just AI" Mindset
**Goal:** Demystify what AI is and start playing with it.

1. **Watch:** An intro video on "What is a Large Language Model?"
2. **Action:** Create a free account on [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) or [Claude](https://claude.ai).
3. **Experiment:** Ask the AI to:
   - "Explain photosynthesis to a 5-year-old."
   - "Give me 5 dinner ideas based on what's in my fridge (eggs, spinach, bread)."
   - "Tell me a joke about robots."

---

## 📅 Week 2: Communicating Effectively
**Goal:** Learn the basics of prompting.

1. **Read:** [The Master Guide to Prompt Engineering](../prompts/master-guide.md) (Intro sections).
2. **Action:** Practice **Role-Playing**. Ask the AI to act as a travel agent, a chef, or a history teacher.
3. **Action:** Use **Chain-of-Thought**. Ask the AI to "Think step-by-step" to solve a logic puzzle.

---

## 📅 Week 3: Beyond Text
**Goal:** Explore images and search.

1. **Action:** Use [Perplexity AI](https://perplexity.ai) to research a topic you're curious about. Notice how it provides sources.
2. **Action:** Try [Canva Magic Media](https://canva.com) or [Microsoft Designer](https://designer.microsoft.com) to generate an image from text.
3. **Reflect:** Think about how these tools change how you find information and create art.

---

## 📅 Week 4: Staying Safe & Informed
**Goal:** Understand hallucinations and ethics.

1. **Understand:** Learn what a "Hallucination" is (when the AI confidently makes things up).
2. **Action:** Test the AI. Ask it about a obscure fact or a fake person and see if it catches itself or makes it up.
3. **Read:** A basic guide on AI Ethics and why you shouldn't share private data with chatbots.
