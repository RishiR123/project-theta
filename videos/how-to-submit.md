# 🎬 Video Submission Guide

Project Theta uses a human-curated pipeline to bridge the gap between open-source collaboration and the YouTube platform.

---

## 🚀 The Workflow

### 1. Create Your Video
- **Format:** MP4 or MOV preferred.
- **Resolution:** 1080p or higher.
- **Philosophy:** Ensure it follows the "It's Just AI" ethos (no jargon, practical, approachable).
- **Credit:** Include your name/social handle at the start or end if you wish to be credited visually.

### 2. Prepare Your Submission
- Host the video file on Google Drive, Dropbox, or as an unlisted YouTube video.
- Ensure the link is accessible.

### 3. Submit the Form
Fill out our [Google Form Submission Link] (Coming Soon). You will need to provide:
- Video Title & Description.
- Your Name/GitHub Handle for credit.
- The link to your video file.
- Confirmation that you agree to the CC BY 4.0 licensing.

### 4. Review & Publication
- The maintainers will review the video for quality and accuracy.
- If approved, it will be uploaded to the official Project Theta YouTube channel.
- You will receive a notification and full credit in the YouTube description and GitHub index.
